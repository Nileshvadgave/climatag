Climate Agnostic Onion Storage Structure

The Climate Smart Onion Storage Structure is an innovative, modular solution designed to extend onion shelf life by 3–6 months through precise micro-climate control using IoT technology. This affordable, farmgate storage system mitigates climatic uncertainties, reducing post-harvest losses from biological factors (rotting, sprouting and Physiological weight loss) by up to 50% compared to the traditional onion storage and also minimizes mechanical damage through smart stacking designs. Its versatile structure supports multiple farm and non-farm commercial uses, enhancing utility and return on investment for farmer. An energy-efficient control algorithm minimizes operational costs, leveraging solar-powered IoT sensors to maintain optimal conditions (25–30°C, 60–70% humidity) with minimal environmental impact.

Storage Features

The system uses a climate-resilient design and IoT-based monitoring to maintain precise storage conditions, protecting onions from humidity and heat stress. Real-time sensing, remote control, and smart alerts ensure timely intervention and operational reliability. A built-in curing module enhances shelf life by reducing moisture and sprouting. Semi-automated handling minimizes labour, mechanical damage, and improves efficiency.

Optimised Micro-climate Control

Shields onions from climatic uncertainties like untimely rain (high humidity), excessive heat (Low humidity) using robust, weather-resistant materials and algorithm based controlled micro-climate to maintain precise micro-climate reducing spoilage risks.

IoT-Enabled Monitoring and Control

Real-time logging of temperature, humidity, and air quality via s IoT sensors, enabling precise micro-climate control and remote system control of Air treatment unit. Farmer don’t need to be at the storage site all the time.

Smart Stacking system

Features a semi-automated bin system that minimizes human intervention, reducing labour costs and mechanical damage while improving operational efficiency.

Onion Curing System

Incorporates a pre-storage curing module to uniformly preheat onions, enhancing shelf life by reducing moisture content and neck closing and preventing biological losses like rotting and sprouting during storage.
