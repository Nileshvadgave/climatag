Scientific Storage Architecture

CAOSS isn't just a shed—it's a precision-engineered micro-environment designed to fight the primary causes of onion rot: Heat and Humidity.

Airflow Engineering

Advanced ventilation paths ensure zero "hot spots," maintaining uniform temperature across all stacking levels.

Thermal Moderation

Humidity Regulation

Constant monitoring keeps Relative Humidity at 60-70%, inhibiting microbial growth and sprouting.
